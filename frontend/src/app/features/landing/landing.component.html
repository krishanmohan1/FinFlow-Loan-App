What you need

Documents we ask for

Identity proof

  • Aadhaar card
  • PAN card
  • Passport (optional)

Income proof

  • Last 3 salary slips
  • Bank statement (6 months)
  • Income tax return

Address proof

  • Utility bill
  • Rental agreement
  • Aadhaar (if address matches)

Loan-specific

  • Property papers (Home loan)
  • Admission letter (Education)
  • Vehicle quotation (Auto)
